⚡ The Go-Go Years Matter

The breakeven analysis shows when total dollars equal out — but which years you receive those dollars matters just as much. Claiming at 62 means more money during your go-go years (65–75), when you can actually enjoy it. Claiming at 70 means a bigger check but zero income for 4–8 years — during the years you need it most.
